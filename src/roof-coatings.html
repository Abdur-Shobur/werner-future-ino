Roof Coatings

Commercial Roof Coatings – New England Region

Roof coatings are a great mechanism to combat the excessive heat of the hot summer and the cooling temperatures of winter in the New England Region. From warehouses, retail outlets and industrial facilities, roof coatings offer a low maintenance, durable, and energy efficient solution for property owners. Commercial elastomeric roof coatings can accommodate all sizes of commercial or industrial buildings. Our New England Commercial Roofing Contractors have installed various forms of coatings to thousands of roofs throughout the New England region.

Elastomeric Roof Coatings and Commercial Buildings

Thanks to the elastic properties, elastomeric roof coatings alter with weather and temperature. The effect is a positive effort to reduction in utility bills and savings for energy. These white coatings that are applied to the roof provide elastic properties that contract and expand with changes in the weather in order to reflect heat from the sun.

Roof coatings are a long-standing solution in to protect commercial roofs from wearing out rapidly under the withering heat of the New England summers. There are different types of roof coatings with varying characteristics that can compliment or increase the longevity of your roofing system. Some kinds can even extend the life of your commercial roof by giving it an additional 20-30 years. Our commercial roofers have applied coatings to thousands of flat roofs and metal roofs for building owners.

Understanding Benefits of Roof Coatings

If you have the ambition to can extend the life of your flat or metal roof, then consider applying elastomeric coatings. Savvy business owners have relied on this method as a worthy investment. If professionally applied, these coatings can offer protection to your roof for years to come, and they’re easily installed and maintained. Elastomeric roof coatings protect against UV rays, sunlight, and moisture, which can increase the lifespan of the roofing system. When it comes to coatings for your roof, it’s important to work with an experienced Massachusetts Commercial Roofing Contractor. There are many benefits that you will observe for your commercial or industrial building:

  • Elastomeric white roofs reflect heat and light from the sun
  • Reduce electric bills and save on energy
  • Seals every seam and crack for a leak-free roof
  • Waterproofs the roof and prevents leaks
  • Reduces HVAC equipment wear-and-tear
  • Ease of maintenance and re-application of fresh coatings
  • Extends the life of your roof
